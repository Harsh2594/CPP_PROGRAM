
Go to your GitHub repository.

Click on Settings (⚙️ tab in the repo).

On the left sidebar, click Collaborators and teams (in Access section).

Click Add people.

Enter their GitHub usernames or email IDs.

Choose their permission level:

Read → can only view/clone.

Write → can push code.

Admin → full control of repo.

Click Add collaborator → They’ll get an email invitation to accept.
